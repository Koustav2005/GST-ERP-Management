📋 HOW IT WORKS:

MANAGEMENT SIGNUP:
  1. Select role: "Management (Company)"
  2. NEW: "Company Name" field appears
  3. Enter: "SVCE Industries"
  4. Sign up
  5. Company created automatically!

OTHER ROLES SIGNUP:
  1. Select role: "Accounts", "Worker", etc.
  2. NEW: "Select Company" dropdown appears
  3. Choose from existing companies
  4. Sign up
  5. Linked to selected company!
